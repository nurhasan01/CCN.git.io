how it works

Search for Universities & Programs

Select the programs and universities of your choice, create your profile and start applying. The application will be reviewed by our experts before it is handed over to the partner universities

Submit your University Application

The University will review your application and if accepted, you will be getting your Letter Of Acceptance from the University. You've crossed the half way mark now in your study abroad journey.
